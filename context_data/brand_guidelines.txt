# Brand Guidelines & Ideas

## Vibe
- Laid back, patio friendly, dog friendly.
- Upper middle class but unpretentious.
- Focused on community, sports, and good weather vibes.

## Party Ideas
- "Dog Days of Summer" (Pool parties for dogs)
- "Heights Night Market" (Local vendors)
- "Retro Jersey Night"
- "Steak Night" (Thursdays)

## Tone
- Fun, energetic, welcoming, but operationally tight.
